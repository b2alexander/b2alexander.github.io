Ms. Ruth Binari

Ms. Ruth Binari or simply Ms. Ruth is the music teacher in Springfield School. This loving, exciting, fun teacher has an excellent background in music. Graduated with major in music studies, this proves that Ms. Ruth's love for music is abundant. She is going to share her love to all her students through her fun and cooperative classes. This class is sure full of fun and surprises.
